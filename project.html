Hotel Reservation

(description)

This system is all about hotel reservations. Users need to log in to their accounts to browse available rooms and select the one they want. Once they find a suitable room, they can proceed with the payment process. The system ensures a seamless and secure experience for users, allowing them to easily book their stay and manage their reservations. Additionally, users can view detailed information about each room, including amenities and pricing, to make an informed decision. The system aims to provide a convenient and efficient way for guests to plan and book their hotel stays.
